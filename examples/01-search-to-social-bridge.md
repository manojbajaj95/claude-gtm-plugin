# The Search-to-Social Bridge

Turn trending YouTube questions into high-performing LinkedIn content by anchoring your post in what your audience is already searching for.

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Find trending questions

```
Topic: [Insert Topic]
Goal: Identify the top 3 "How-to" questions currently trending as video titles or
common audience questions on YouTube. Note the comment themes for the #1 result.
```

### Step 2 — Validate with AEO keywords

```
Take the 3 questions from Step 1 and cross-reference them with high-volume AEO
keywords. Rewrite each question in the phrasing people use when querying AI
assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE). Return the #1 ranked question with
its AEO-optimized phrasing.
```

### Step 3 — Write the LinkedIn post

```
Question to answer: [output from Step 2]
Format: listicle (5–7 numbered points)
Hook: open with a specific pain point pulled from YouTube comments on the #1 video
CTA: invite readers to save the post or drop a follow-up question in the comments
Read strategy/brand.md for voice before drafting.
```

## Output

A single LinkedIn post saved to `content/linkedin/drafts/YYYY-MM-DD_<topic-slug>.md` that:
- Answers a proven high-demand question
- Uses phrasing that also ranks in AI search results
- Opens with a comment-validated pain point to earn the scroll-stop
