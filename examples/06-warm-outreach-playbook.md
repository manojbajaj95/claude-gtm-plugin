# The Warm Outreach Playbook

Build a personalized 3-email outreach sequence for 10 high-value prospects — each opening with a real, recent win specific to their company before connecting it to your value proposition.

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Identify and research prospects

```
Identify 10 high-value prospects in the [Insert Industry] sector.
For each prospect, find one specific recent win or news item (last 90 days):
  - Funding round, acquisition, or product launch
  - Award, press mention, or executive hire
  - Market expansion or public milestone
Return a list of 10 companies with: company name, relevant contact role,
and the specific news item with source.
```

### Step 2 — Personalize the opening line

```
Write a unique, personalized opening line for each
of the 10 prospects based on their news item from Step 1.
Each line should:
  - Reference the specific event naturally (not flattery)
  - Be 1–2 sentences
  - Set up a logical bridge to our value proposition
```

### Step 3 — Write the email sequence

```
Read strategy/brand.md for our core value proposition and ICP.
Using the personalized opening lines from Step 2, draft a 3-email outbound sequence
that works for all 10 prospects (personalized opener + shared body):

Email 1 (Day 1): Personalized opener → 1 sentence on what we do → soft CTA
  (reply to learn more, not "book a call")
Email 2 (Day 4): Follow-up focused on one specific ROI proof point
Email 3 (Day 9): Break-up email with a low-friction offer (resource, insight, or question)

Each email under 150 words. Subject lines included.
```

## Output

- `content/email/drafts/YYYY-MM-DD_outreach-<industry-slug>.md` — full sequence with all 10 personalized openers
