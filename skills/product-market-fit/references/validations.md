# Product Market Fit - Validations

## No PMF Measurement

### **Id**
no-pmf-measurement
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Product-market fit should be measured, not assumed
### **Indicators**
  - No PMF survey conducted
  - No retention analysis
### **Message**
PMF assumed but not measured.
### **Fix Action**
Run PMF survey and analyze retention cohorts

## Vanity Metrics as PMF

### **Id**
vanity-pmf
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Growth does not equal PMF; retention does
### **Indicators**
  - Citing signups as PMF evidence
  - No retention data
### **Message**
Using growth metrics instead of retention for PMF.
### **Fix Action**
Focus on retention metrics, not acquisition

## Scaling Without PMF

### **Id**
scaling-before-pmf
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Significant growth investment should wait for PMF
### **Indicators**
  - Heavy marketing spend with poor retention
  - Rapid hiring without PMF evidence
### **Message**
Scaling growth before PMF confirmed.
### **Fix Action**
Confirm PMF with 40%+ survey score or strong retention

## Incorrect PMF Survey Method

medium
